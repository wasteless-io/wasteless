1 — Create the wasteless roles in your AWS account

One CloudFormation stack (or Terraform module) creates wasteless-readonly — Describe/Get/List permissions only. It outputs the values you paste in step 2. If someone on your team manages AWS, send them this link; it is the only part that happens inside your AWS account.

  • CloudFormation (no tooling needed): upload onboarding/cloudformation/ in the AWS console → Create stack
  • Terraform: apply onboarding/terraform/

Full walkthrough: docs/CTO_QUICKSTART.md (short) or docs/AWS_SETUP.md (detailed, per-permission).

2 — Paste the values, test, save

3 — Collect

Once saved, run wasteless collect (or wait for the scheduled collection) and the dashboard fills up. Nothing in your account is ever modified without an explicit approval in Recommendations.
